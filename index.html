Guardians of the Galaxy: Vol. 2 (2017)

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 is a 2017 superhero fantasy adventure film, based on the Marvel Comics superhero team called by the same name as the movie. The film is a sequel to Guardians of the Galaxy. It is the fifteenth film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe” and the third film of ”Phase Three”
Movie description: The Guardians struggle to keep together as a team while dealing with their personal family issues, notably Star-Lord's encounter with his father the ambitious celestial being Ego.

Peter Quill/Star Lord

Christopher "Chris" Michael Pratt born June 21 year 1979. He is an American actor from Virgina Minnesota, U.S. His career started year 2000, but his well known from the series ”Parks and Recreation” (2009-2015). He is also known from the Jurassic World movies (2015, 2018) and of course both of the Guardians of the Galaxy where he plays the leading role as Star-Lord, Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and Avengers: Endgame (2019).

Movie director

James Gunn

James Francis Gunn Jr. born August 5 year 1966 is an American film director, actor, producer and screenwriter. From St. Louis, Missouri, U.S. He began working as a director year 2006 with the horror-comedy Slither (2006), and eventually moving to the superhero genre with Guardians of the Galaxy (2014) and Guardians of the Galaxy (2017). He is currently directing a movie coming in 2021, The Suicide Squad, also know as Suicide Squad 2.

Gamora Zen Whoberi Ben Titan

Played by Zoe Saldana

Zoë Yadira Saldaña Nazario (Zoe Saldana) born June 19 year 1978 in Passaic, New Jersey, U.S. She is an American actress known for many of her roles. She has played Neytiri in the Avatar movie (2009), Nyota Uhura in Star Trek (2009), and of course she is known for playing Gamora in the Guardians of the Galaxy movies ( 2014, 2017), Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and Avengers: Endgame (2019).

Drax the Destroyer

Played by Dave Bautista

David Michael Bautista Jr. (born January 18, 1969) is an American actor, retired professional wrestler, former mixed martial artist and bodybuilder. Bautista began his wrestling career in 1999, and signed with the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) in 2000. David began acting in 2006 and has starred in The Man with the Iron Fists (2012), James Bond Spectre (2015) and Blade Runner 2049 (2017). In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he has played the role of Drax the Destroyer in the films Guardians of the Galaxy (2014), Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017), Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and Avengers: Endgame (2019).
